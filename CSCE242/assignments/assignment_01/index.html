The World of Video Games

Video games, are very quickly becoming the world's preferred pastime.

With extensive genres to choose from, there is something for everyone.

Who wouldn't want to forget their worries and go to another world?

With access and availability growing daily, what is stopping you from trying them out?

How to get Involved

There are many ways to get involved with games.

We used to have to go out to places called arcades to play games.

Now we have games everywhere, any time, with phones,consoles, and computers.

How you decide to play is up to you!

Your Options

  • Arcades
  • Mobile Phones
  • Consoles
  • Personal Computers

The Positives and Negatives

Device Positives Negatives
Arcade Cheapest Not found commonly
Mobile Very accessible Limited game variety
Console Accessible and better priced Limited performance
Personal Computer Highest performing and largest variety Price is related to performance
